About

More About Me.

I am a Seasoned Graphic Designer and enthusiastic Web Developer Novice.

During normal business hours I create all kind of visual stuff for different companies and clients. I help them to make great first impressions online and offline with the power of polished, appealing graphic design.

At nights I learn artificial and natural languages.

One day I hope to return to my beloved forest in the mountains, where I can continue my passion and tend flowers and trees to cultivate beauty.
